Dynamic Boba

It's finally time for the fun stuff!! How do I make this boba meaningfully interactive? What adjustments might someone want to make? What data should we track? Referencing back to my Google Sheets tracker, I made a list of the customizations you might want to track for any boba drink:

  • The actual drink
    • Name/type
      • Drink Colour - Green tea, black tea, strawberry infused tea, etc.
    • Dairy?
      • Drink Colour - A drink with dairy will be paler than one without
      • Drink Opacity - A drink with dairy will also be opaque. Dairy-less teas should be transparent
  • Topping(s)
    • Shape and Colour - Pearls? Mini pearls? Grass jelly? Mango bits? Strawberry compote?
    • Amount - Normal? Light? Extra?
    • How to handle combinations of toppings?
  • Straw
    • Colour - Random from a palette? Accepts user input just for funsies?
    • Width - Normal? Wide? Determined by topping type... but override-able?
  • Ice
    • Shape - Cubes? Crushed?
    • Amount - Normal ice? Light ice? No ice?
  • Data with no visual implications (for the drink itself)
    • Sweetness level
    • Date
    • Store/location
    • Price
    • Rating
    • Reusable straw/cup used

One of the most important visual cues is the drink colour and opacity.
